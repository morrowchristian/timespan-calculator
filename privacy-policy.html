Privacy Policy for Time Span Calculator

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Time Span Calculator is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Collection

No personal data is collected. This extension does not collect any personal information, browsing history, or usage data.

Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage API to remember your time preferences between sessions. This includes:

  • Last used start time
  • Last used end time
  • Last used add minutes value

This data never leaves your computer and is only stored locally on your device.

Permissions

The "storage" permission is used exclusively to save your time preferences locally. No other permissions are required or used.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking technologies.
